Yoko's Kitchen

Yoko's Kitchen is a unique restaurant, where you are offered not only to dine in and enjoy the cuisine with friends/family, but also to be included in the process with cooking classes. We offer a variety of classes with different styles of cooking that you can enjoy after.

Japanese cuisine has long been revered for its unique ingredients, innovative flavors, and distinct sense of culture and history. The best Japanese food tells a story through its creative use of ingredients, shaping the dining experience into something both enjoyable and eye-opening.
